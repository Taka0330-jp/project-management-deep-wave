Activities Overview

Surfing

Ride the Pacific swells and feel the west coast spirit! Just a short trip from Vancouver, Tofino’s sandy beaches and consistent waves offer the ultimate surf experience for all levels. From beginner lessons to advanced sessions, surfing here combines adventure, nature, and the laid-back coastal lifestyle that defines British Columbia’s surf culture.

Jet Skiing

Experience Vancouver from a thrilling new angle, on the water! Jet skiing through English Bay and False Creek gives you breathtaking views of the skyline and mountains while you feel the rush of the open sea. Perfect for friends, families, or adventure seekers, our jet ski experiences blend speed, freedom, and fun in one unforgettable ride.
