FAQs

What are the best water activities to try in Vancouver?

Vancouver offers a wide range of water adventures! Some of the most popular include scuba diving in Howe Sound, jet skiing around English Bay, paddle boarding at Kitsilano Beach, and surfing in nearby Tofino. Whether you’re a beginner or an expert, there’s something for everyone.

Do I need previous experience to join these activities?

Not at all! Most of our activities are beginner-friendly. Our certified instructors will guide you through all the basics before you start, so you can feel safe and confident even if it’s your first time on the water.

What equipment is provided?

We provide all the essential gear for each activity — including wetsuits, life jackets, snorkels, or diving equipment depending on the experience you choose. Just bring your swimsuit, towel, and excitement!

Are the activities available year-round?

Most activities run from spring to early fall, when the weather and water are perfect. However, some indoor or guided options like scuba diving lessons are available year-round depending on conditions.
