Surfing

Dash across the sea with speed and excitement.

If you’re looking for an adrenaline-pumping way to explore Vancouver’s coastline, jet skiing is the perfect choice. Imagine speeding across crystal waters with the city skyline behind you and the North Shore Mountains ahead, it’s the perfect mix of nature and excitement. Vancouver’s marinas and beaches offer easy access to jet ski rentals, making it simple for visitors and locals alike to hit the water.

Popular launch spots include English Bay, False Creek, and Coal Harbour, where you can start your adventure right from downtown. Guided jet ski tours often take you along the shores of Stanley Park, under the Lions Gate Bridge, and even toward Bowen Island. Along the way, you might spot seals, eagles, or even dolphins if you’re lucky!

For thrill-seekers, jet skiing is one of the most exciting ways to experience Vancouver’s coastal views. The combination of speed, freedom, and scenery makes it a favourite summer activity among locals. Some companies even offer sunset rides, where you can watch the sky turn orange as you glide across the calm evening waters.

So, if you’re craving a unique way to explore the city and feel the rush of the Pacific, jet skiing is your go-to adventure. Just bring sunscreen, sunglasses, and your sense of adventure, Vancouver’s waters are waiting!
