About us

Our Story

Born from a passion for the ocean, DeepWave Explorers began with one simple idea, to share Vancouver’s breathtaking marine world with everyone.

Our certified instructors and local guides combine years of experience with a deep respect for nature, creating adventures that are safe, exciting, and unforgettable. Whether you’re diving into the depths of Howe Sound or gliding across English Bay, we make every moment count beneath the waves.

Our Activities

From calm paddles to thrilling dives, explore the best of Vancouver’s waters.
We offer guided scuba diving, jet ski tours, paddle boarding lessons, and surf trips to Tofino, tailored to all skill levels.
Gear up and let the sea surprise you.
